Q & A — CruZionsville

What is CruZionsville?
It is an event in which Porsche owners from five states gather together to see rare and unusual Porsche cars as well as the newest models. The proceeds from this event go to the Alzheimer’s Organization of Central Indiana.

How many years has this event taken place?
This is the fifth year. The crowds keep getting larger and larger and we continue to look for new ways to keep it exciting. We have some surprises for you again this year as well.

How many cars will there be?
We expect 125 Porsche cars on the brick street in downtown Zionsville. We also will be displaying some beautiful motorcycles from Ducati!

What kind of entertainment will be at the event?
There will be a DJ and live entertainment, so look for the listing. There will be a People’s Choice judging with the public helping decide the most beautiful of the cars on display.

Is there something for kids to do at the event?
Yes! The kids’ area is open from 10:45 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. It is located next to the Brick Street Inn. Painting canvas and materials will be provided by the Sullivan Muncie Cultural Center. Each child will have an opportunity to “give back” a donation to the Alzheimer's Organization of Central Indiana for having fun at CruZionsville.

Tell us about the Fashion Show!
Beautiful clothes will be shown by our local dress shops along Main Street. This outdoor fashion show will begin promptly at 1 p.m. You won’t want to miss being on the brick street to catch up on what clothing trends are in for spring and summer.

Are there places to eat during CruZionsville?
Zionsville offers a wide range of wonderful dining options — from salads and sandwiches to sushi — so bring the whole family for a day in Zionsville. There is so much to see and do.

Why did the Porsche Car Club of Indiana choose to donate its proceeds to the nonprofit Alzheimer’s Association?
Alzheimer’s is a debilitating disease with no cure that has adversely affected so many families. We wanted to create more exposure for this worthwhile cause while at the same time providing resources to help our community.

Can anyone make a donation?
Absolutely! There is a link at www.cruzionsville.com to the Alzheimer’s Association’s website. Our goal is to raise $15,000 between the sponsorships, car registrations, and our other activities. We have specially numbered CruZionsville bottles of wine for sale. You can make a donation to enter into a drawing for a chance to win a Man's Watch (thanks to Goodman’s Jewelers). We have special Porsche 911 custom coffee mugs filled with ground coffee, popcorn, or chocolate for a donation of just $15. Don’t forget our beautiful custom-designed polos and T-shirts as well as Inga’s special CruZionsville Popcorn, created just for this event!

Come and enjoy the fun.

Motor On!

Stephen Tarr, Event Chair
